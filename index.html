Controls

Shift - Left Click --> Move camera

Left Click --> Rotate camera around orbit

Scroll Wheel --> Zoom in and out

Space --> Render current Scene and display on view frustum

a --> toggle showing all or one ray from view frustum

Arrow Keys --> move the selected pixel on the view frustum in display window

Number Keys

0 - Scene 0 = Cornell Box
1 - Scene 1 = 3 spheres with no shadowing

3 - Scene 3 = spheres and planes, multiple lights
4 - Scene 4 = mirror balls with a single light bounce
5 - Scene 5 = mirror bounce with multiple bounces
6 - Scene 6 = Bunny (OBJ) zoomed out (quick render)
7 - Scene 7 = Bunny (OBJ) zoomed in (longer time to render)
8 - Scene 8 = Utah TeaPot (OBJ)
9 - Scene 9 = Robot (OBJ)
