Controls

Shift - Left Click --> Move camera

Left Click --> Rotate camera around orbit

Scroll Wheel --> Zoom in and out

Arrow Keys --> move the selected pixel on the view frustum in display window

Number Keys

0 - Scene 0 = rotating animation of eva and teapot to show realtime view window is active

1 - Scene 1 = Cornell Box

2 - Scene 2 = Bunny(OBJ) and Cylinder(primitive) with light in scene space12

3 - Scene 3 = Eva Unit 1(OBJ)

4 - Scene 4 = Utah TeaPot(OBJ)

5 - Scene 5 = Bunny(OBJ) and Cylinder(primitive) with light locked to camera

6 - Scene 6 = Bunny(OBJ) zoomed out

7 - Scene 7 = Bunny(OBJ) Closer
